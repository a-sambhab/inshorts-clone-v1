Bezos' flight set to be world's 1st unpiloted civilian space flight
short by Hiral Goyal / 11.01 pm on 15 Jul 2021, Thursday
World's richest person Jeff Bezos' upcoming flight to space is set to be the world's first unpiloted suborbital flight with an all-civilian crew. The four-person crew including Bezos will fly aboard Blue Origin's 60-foot-tall zNew Shepard spacecraft that is fully autonomous and cannot be piloted from inside. The 11-minute ride will take them above 100 km, or the Kármán line.

WhatsApp banned 20 lakh Indian accounts from May 15 to June 15
short by Pratik Swagat / 11.01 pm on 15 Jul 2021, Thursday
